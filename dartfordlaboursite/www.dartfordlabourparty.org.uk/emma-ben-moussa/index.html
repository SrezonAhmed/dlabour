Emma Ben Moussa

Emma Ben Moussa is Swanscombe’s Labour Borough Councillor, first elected in May 2019.

Emma is also Swanscombe’s Labour Councillor on Swanscombe and Greenhithe Town Council, and the sole opposition Councillor to the Swanscombe and Greenhithe Residents Association.

Emma lives in Swanscombe with her family and since being elected has been a strong voice for Swanscombe on the Council, securing new litter bins across Swanscombe to help combat littering, helping get new benches installed in the local cemetery and also organising community events to bring the community together.

Emma has also campaigned against the Grove Car Park charges and is currently working for CCTV to be installed across Swanscombe and for more enforcement officers to stop the dangerous parking in the area.
